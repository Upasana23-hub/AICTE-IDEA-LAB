Equipments

Laser cutting machine

These machines can precisely cut and engrave various materials, enabling students to create intricate designs and prototypes. The USP includes high precision and versatility in material choices. Technical Assembly and Workshop on CO2 Laser Cutting Machine


3d printer

These can rapidly create prototypes of designs, allowing students to physically visualize and test their ideas. The USP here lies in the ability to quickly iterate and refine designs.

Cnc wood cutter

Computer Numerical Control (CNC) machines are used for automated manufacturing processes, such as milling, drilling, and cutting. Their USP lies in their ability to produce complex shapes with high accuracy.

vinyl cutting machine

Vinyl cutters can work with a wide range of materials, such as vinyl sheets, heat transfer vinyl (HTV), adhesive-backed vinyl, cardstock, paper, fabric, and more. This versatility makes them suitable for various applications, including signage, decals, apparel design, and craft projects.
